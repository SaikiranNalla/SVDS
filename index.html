About SVDS

Established in 2009 in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, SVDS Goods Transport has grown into a trusted name in the logistics and transportation industry. With over a decade of experience, we have proudly served a diverse range of industrial clients, delivering consignments efficiently and safely across India and even to international destinations such as Bhutan, Nepal, and Bangladesh.

Our head office in Visakhapatnam acts as the central hub for coordinating seamless logistics operations throughout the country. We specialize in transporting a wide variety of industrial materials and have handled consignments of all sizes and complexities.
